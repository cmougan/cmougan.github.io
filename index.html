Previous Position

Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellow(June 2017 - June 2019)

Department of Multi-Agent Systems, IIIA-CSIC, Barcelona, Spain

Project: Collectiveware: Highly-Parallel Algorithms for Collective Intelligence (HPA4CF)

Funding: € 158 121 (H2020-MSCA-IF-2016)

Abstract: The aim of the project is to design and evaluate novel highly-parallel algorithms for real-world Collective Intelligence domains, which usually translate into large-scale (online) Combinatorial Optimisation problems. As prominent examples of these domains we consider shared mobility and cooperative learning. Result show that the implementation of optimal policies within these domain can produce major societal benefits, by reducing pollution and energy consumption by up to 70.78%, and by promoting productivity and learning by 25.3%, respectively.
